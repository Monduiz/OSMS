Awesome features

The Officer Safety Management System (OSMS) is a response to the objectives of the Officer Safety Initiative in implementing a data strategy in order to become a data-driven organization. As such, Health and safety incidents, aspects of operations can be captured to build a better understanding of risks and empower the officers and the branch with knowelegde to enhance preparedness and safety. OSMS is a microservices and open source project that can grow with the organization.

Open source

OSMS is built using open source technologies and powered by Django, Mapbox, PostgreSQL and PostGIS.

Microservices

Instead of onerous enterprise solutions, business needs are cut into smaller parts that work together. Responsive, adaptive and grows as needed. Low risks and costs.

Responsive layouts

Built with an interoperable design, it works on all screen sizes and devices. Provides an equivalent experience on desktops, phones and tablets.

Analytics

Dashboards, customized information for officers and managers. Databases: safety issues, incidents, activities, locations, places. Reporting and analysis.

Stewardship

A product from the Enforcement Branch, built by the Enforcement Branch. Investing in people, not in software empowers.

APIs

APIs make data accessible to other systems and to people. This promotes the sharing and reuse of data to empower the branch and work in the open as default.
